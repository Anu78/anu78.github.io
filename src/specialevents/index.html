Special Events

  • Intro To Calc 10-14 August, 2020

    We are offering a free, livestreamed course on Zoom for students starting AP Calculus this schoolyear. However, all interested students are welcome to join!

    From 2:00 pm - 3:15 pm (ET) every day from August 10th through August 14th, we will be reviewing essential Precalculus concepts and introducing basic Calculus concepts. Entering AP Calculus with a conceptual understanding will greatly benefit those wishing to succeed in the class.

    Here is the topic for each day's stream:
    Aug. 10 - Algebra: Zoom Link
    Aug. 11 - Functions
    Aug. 12 - Unit Circle
    Aug. 13 - Analytic Trig
    Aug. 14 - Limits and Derivatives

    The Zoom links will be posted right here. If you can't attend, fear not! The recorded sessions will also be posted here for you to review at a later time.
